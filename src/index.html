About

We aim to inspire and uplift by integrating the arts with care, enriching lives and celebrating every individual's unique contributions.

SHINGENMAX seamlessly blends creativity with compassionate care, excelling in art operations, performing arts support, and exceptional residential care for the elderly and disabled. Through innovative artistic endeavors and heartfelt caregiving, we foster a vibrant environment that uplifts and enriches our diverse community.

  • Expertise in art operations, performing arts support, and residential care.
  • Fostering a harmonious environment where creativity and care intersect.
  • Enhancing lives through inspiration, belonging, and enrichment.

Services

Community Engagement

Hosting workshops and events to promote arts appreciation and connect residents with local artists and performers.

Performing Arts Support

Supporting performances with logistical assistance and resources for rehearsals and events.

Art Operations

Curating and promoting art exhibitions while supporting creative projects that engage the community.

Residential Care Services

Providing comprehensive care for the elderly and individuals with disabilities, including daily support and creative activities.
